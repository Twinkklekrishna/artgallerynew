Digital Horizons

Artist: Ava Rodriguez

Year: 2022

"Digital Horizons" is a captivating blend of abstract shapes and vibrant colors created entirely with generative algorithms. The piece explores the intersection of technology and imagination, evoking futuristic landscapes and emotional depth through code-based art.
